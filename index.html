Who I Am

I am an astrophysics researcher focused on stellar magnetic activity and star–planet interactions. Affiliated with the University of Potsdam and the Leibniz Institute for Astrophysics Potsdam (AIP), my work integrates observational analysis, numerical modeling, and software development to advance the understanding of stellar dynamo processes and the impact of planetary engulfment events.

Current Research Areas

  • Stellar magnetic activity and planetary engulfment
  • Exoplanet-star magnetic interactions in wide binary systems
  • Astrophotonic component fabrication & testing (e.g., photonic lanterns)

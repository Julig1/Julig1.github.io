Who I Am

I am a passionate astrophysics researcher specializing in stellar magnetic activity and exoplanet interactions. Based at the University of Potsdam & AIP, I blend scientific inquiry with software development to push the boundaries of stellar astrophysics.

Current Research Areas

  • Stellar magnetic activity and planetary engulfment
  • Exoplanet-star magnetic interactions in wide binary systems
  • Astrophotonic component fabrication & testing (e.g., photonic lanterns)
